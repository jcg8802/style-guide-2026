Trend 01

Soft Tailoring

Unstructured blazers in linen, cotton, and textured fabrics — worn open over a clean tee or fine knit. The Goldilocks silhouette: not boxy, not slim. Just right.

Key Brands

Zegna Brunello Cucinelli Drake's Loewe

Palette

Stone · Sand · Khaki · Ivory · Soft Olive

The Move

"Pull a stone linen blazer over a white tee and navy straight-legs — leave it open, roll the sleeves twice. You look effortless. You look expensive."

Trend 02

Linen Sets &
Resort Wear

Matching linen shirt + trouser in the same tone. Italian vacation dressing that doubles as investor dinner. The surest signal that you've figured it out.

Key Brands

Loro Piana Jacquemus Massimo Alba Corridor

Footwear

Leather Sandals Minimalist Loafers

Palette

Ecru · Clay · Sage · Warm White · Terracotta

The Move

"Buy the set. Wear the set. Add leather sandals. Don't overthink it — coordinated dressing this clean is the whole point."

Trend 03

Straight-Leg
Trousers

The pendulum swings back from baggy — settling at clean, straight, and intentional. Single-pleat options coming in strong: relaxed through the thigh, clean at the break.

Key Brands

Incotex Brioni Ring Jacket De Bonne Facture

Fabrics

Linen Cotton Twill Light Wool

Palette

Navy · Tan · Olive · Ivory · Mid-Grey

The Move

"Single-pleat linen trousers in navy or tan — with a relaxed tuck at the front. Pair with loafers, no socks. You're not trying, and everyone knows you tried."

Trend 04

Elevated
Knits

Fine-gauge cashmere and merino crewnecks and half-zips replacing the dress shirt for smart-casual. The most sophisticated upgrade you can make to any outfit.

Key Brands

Brunello Cucinelli Loro Piana John Smedley

Palette

Camel · Navy · Off-White · Mid-Grey

The Move

"Camel cashmere crewneck over a white tee, tucked lightly into straight navy trousers. Add loafers. You've just outperformed everyone in a boardroom without wearing a tie."

Trend 05

Quiet Luxury
Polos & Tees

Premium Pima cotton and mercerized cotton in clean, logo-free cuts. The foundation of every great outfit this season. Get the fit right and it says everything.

Key Brands

Zegna Kiton Fedeli Sunspel

Palette

White · Ecru · Pale Blue · Sage · Black

The Move

"White Sunspel tee under the linen blazer. Tucked. Crease-free. It's not boring — it's the whole look."

Trend 06

The Loafer
Takeover

Penny loafers and horsebit loafers are dominating smart-casual. Suede for day, polished leather for evening. With or without socks — both valid, both intentional.

Key Brands

Gucci Tod's Baudoin & Lange Paraboot

Palette

Tan Suede · Dark Brown · Cognac · Black

The Move

"Tan suede Baudoin & Lange slipper loafers with cropped linen trousers, no socks. It's the shoe that makes strangers ask where you shop."

Trend 07

Minimalist
Leather Sneakers

Clean white or off-white leather court sneakers — premium alternatives to the logo-heavy mainstream. The move when loafers feel too formal, dress shoes feel too much.

Key Brands

Common Projects Axel Arigato New Balance 550 Veja

Styling

Tailored Trousers Linen Sets Shorts

The Move

"Common Projects Achilles in white with straight-leg khaki trousers and a linen shirt. The sneaker that signals you're past logo culture."

Trend 08

Technical
Outerwear

Nylon and technical field jackets worn over tailored pieces. Lightweight, packable, weather-ready — functionality that doesn't apologize for looking this good.

Key Brands

Zegna (Technical) C.P. Company Veilance

Palette

Navy · Black · Stone · Olive

The Move

"Stone Veilance field jacket over a cashmere crewneck and tailored trousers. It says: I can go from a board meeting to the airport in ten minutes."

Trend 09

Summer
Shirting

Open-collar linen and gauze shirts with clean construction. Camp collar or classic — both are right. Worn untucked or half-tucked, never fully buttoned, always intentional.

Key Brands

Gitman Vintage Drake's Inis Meáin Corridor

Palette

White · Pale Blue · Thin Stripe · Subtle Check

The Move

"Drake's linen shirt in white or pale blue, one button open at the neck, sleeves rolled to the elbow. Half-tuck if you're wearing trousers. This is the shirt that does 70% of the work."
